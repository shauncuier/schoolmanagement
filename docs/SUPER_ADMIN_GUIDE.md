## üè´ Schools Management
Manage the life-cycle of schools on the platform.

### Registering a New School
1. Go to **Schools Management** > **Add School**.
2. Fill in the **Basic Info** and **Address**.
3. **Branding**: Choose the school's theme colors. Use the **Live Preview** to ensure a premium look.
4. **Subscription**: Assign an initial plan (Basic, Standard, etc.).
5. **Status**: Set to "Active" to enable immediate access.

### Changing Brand Colors
You can update a school's colors at any time. These changes apply instantly to the school's specific dashboard and sidebar.

## üë• Global User Management
The **All Users** section allows you to manage users across all tenants.
- **Search**: Find any user by name or email globaly.
- **Filter**: Narrow down by school, role, or account status.
- **Status Control**: Suspend or activate accounts.
- **Soft Delete**: Users who are deleted can be found in the "Deleted" filter and **Restored** or permanently removed.

---

## üí≥ Subscriptions & Billing
Monitor and manage school revenue plans.
- **Update Plan**: Switch a school between Free, Basic, Standard, and Premium tiers.
- **Extend Expiry**: Add days to a school's current subscription end date.
- **Cancel**: Revert a school to the Free tier.
