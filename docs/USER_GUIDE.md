### Accessing the Platform
1. Navigate to the school's URL (e.g., `your-school.schoolsync.com`).

3. If configured, complete the Two-Factor Authentication (2FA).

### Roles & Responsibilities
SchoolSync behaves differently depending on your role:
- **Super Admin**: Platform management, school registration, and system settings.
- **School Admin**: Full control over a specific school's operations.
- **Teacher**: Manage classes, attendance, and grading.
- **Student/Parent**: View academic progress, attendance, and pay fees.

### Dashboard Overview
Your dashboard provides a real-time summary of academic health:
- **Attendance Today**: Visual tracking of present/absent students.
- **Academic Stats**: Counts of students, teachers, and classes.
- **Actionable Links**: Shortcut buttons to common tasks like "Add Student" or "New Notice".

### Updating Your Profile
1. Click on your name in the sidebar or header.
2. Select **Settings**.
3. Update your profile info, change your password, or switch to dark mode.
